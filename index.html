Why Jazz Coin?

Thanks to Decentralized Finance (DeFi), Jazz Coin offers users the ability to earn money by Staking, Pre-Sale and Airdrops on our single platform without risk of losing their funds. The security of our users' funds is a biggest priority for all our team.

We believe that our user is main active for our success. This belief naturally means the importance of providing quality decentralized financial services, security, transparency and ease of use.

DISTRIBUTION

Initial Jazz Coin will be distributed during a pre-sale event, during which a part of received BNB will be swapped for Jazz Coin giving the project its first "price pump". After the pre-sale ends, undistributed Jazz Coin will be distributed between users as a one-time subsidy. As marked earlier, Jazz Coin doesn't have mining capability, the Jazz cap (1,500,000,000) is fixed forever. There is no way to release more Jazz Coin. Undistributed Jazz Coin will be used to add liquidity to other DEX platforms such as PancakeSwap, and some of them will be handed out as Airdrop to first investors and media partners and some will be locked forever.
